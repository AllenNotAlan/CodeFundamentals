# Monzo Engineering Role Assessment

## Monzo Minimum Requirements (Mid/Senior Backend Engineer)

- Strong backend experience on a technology product
- Some experience with strongly-typed languages (Go, Java, C, Scala, etc.)
- Interest in distributed systems and writing resilient software
- Comfortable working in ambiguous team environments
- Product mindset — data-informed decisions, customer outcomes
- No degree required, no specific stack knowledge required

**Salary:** Mid ~£73k–£95k | Senior (L50) £95k–£130k + incentive awards + stock options

## My Strengths

- ✅ 4+ years backend experience (Aflac & FinTrU)
- ✅ Strongly-typed language experience (C#)
- ✅ Product mindset — customer-facing auth/fraud detection systems
- ✅ Distributed systems exposure — APIs, authentication services
- ✅ Comfortable with ambiguity — small teams, multiple responsibilities
- ✅ Standout: award-winning FIDO2/passkey implementation

## Potential Gaps

- Monzo's stack: Go, Cassandra, Kafka, Kubernetes (they train on onboarding, but familiarity helps)
- Emphasis on "resilient software" and "distributed systems" — need to articulate in system design terms (failure modes, retries, idempotency, eventual consistency)

## Actions to Maximise Chances for Senior (L50)

- Prepare system design answers (auth systems, fraud detection pipelines — real experience)
- Quantify impact (users affected, latency improvements, fraud prevented)
- Brush up on Go basics — even a side project shows initiative
- Be ready to discuss trade-offs in distributed systems

## Interview Process

1. Initial call
2. Take-home task or pair coding
3. Final: system design + behavioural (~4 weeks total)
